Under the hood
Private by design

Nothing leaves your machine

It's literally watching your screen, so privacy isn't a setting — it's the default. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is phoned home.

Privacy & security, by default

🏠
100% local
All processing on your machine. Zero network calls after the initial model download. No telemetry, ever.
🛡️
Sensitive-data filter
Auto-redacts credit cards (Luhn-checked), SSNs, API keys, JWTs and passwords from captured text before storage.
🔐
Encryption at rest
Optional AES (Fernet) encryption for screenshots, with the key held in your OS keyring.
🔢
Dashboard PIN lock
Hashed PIN with a configurable auto-lock timeout for the dashboard.
🕶️
Incognito mode
One-click pause — nothing is recorded while it's on.
🗑️
Retention control
Auto-delete data older than N days (or keep forever). Clear any day from the timeline instantly.
Fun fact: the secret-redaction pass runs before the model ever sees the frame — so even your own local AI never reads your card numbers, SSNs or API keys.
